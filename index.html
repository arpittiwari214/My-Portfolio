Case Study: Bade Bhaiya

An AI-powered career guidance platform.

The Problem

Many students struggle to choose a career path due to a lack of personalized guidance. The goal was to create a tool that could offer intelligent, data-driven career recommendations.

My Role & Process

As the lead developer, I was responsible for the full-stack development and UI/UX design. The process involved initial wireframing in Figma, developing a component-based frontend in React, and engineering the backend recommendation logic in Python.

The Outcome

The final product is a platform that matches students to careers with over 90% accuracy based on quiz data. This project solidified my skills in full-stack development and practical AI implementation.
